 Motive of JUST SUPPORT  

          
Our website will be helpful in following ways---
 
        1.If any kind hearted person see a needy people on road or any other place .
          He/She may register his/her details in our website and we will transfer these details 
          to nearby NGO for help of that person and provide him/her there support and
          register them in some government scheme for needy people.

        2.If any person is jobless then he/she can register his/her details "ENTER SKILL FOR GET JOB" and
          we link them to our"PROVIDE JOB" section and he/she will be contacted by desired person 
          who release vacanies in "PROVIDE JOB" coloumn.

        3.If anyone see a child doing labouring .Then he/she can complain about it by providing child 
          details in our respective section and we will contact to child welfare association and police of
          that area for that child help.The identity of person providing details is keep secret.
